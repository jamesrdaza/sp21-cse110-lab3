My Bike Ride

4/9/2021

After a long night of no sleep I woke up and realized I had not eaten anything the day before. I was hungry I hastily decided to go for a quick bike ride to get some chicken wings. The more and more I pedaled the more tired I had gotten. About halfway through my chicken wing journey I was exhausted chalking it upto lack of excercise due to the pandemic. This was the most tired I've been biking around the UCSD hills even the employee noticed how winded I was and commented on it. It was then when I had some suspicions so I inspected my bike and realized there was a flat tire. In my hungry craze for wings I failed to notice that my front bike tire was flat. Then I had 2 choices to either walk my bike or ride it on the way back. My hungry mind was flooded at the thought of eating those chicken wings so I decided to just bear it out and here I am today with a broken front tire and can't bike until I get it fixed now.

    Things to do:
  • Figure out what inner tube I have
  • Get new inner tube
  • Learn how to replace inner tube
